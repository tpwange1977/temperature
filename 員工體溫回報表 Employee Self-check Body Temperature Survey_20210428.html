Question Title

* 4. 是否有以下任一狀況, 若有請立即更新健康聲明書
Do you have any following conditions? If yes, please re-fill the health declaration form

1. 近14日內是否曾有出國紀錄? Have you ever gone abroad in the past 14 days?
2. 近14日內是否為中央流行疫情指揮中心匡列為居家隔離者? Have you received a quarantine notification from CECC in the last 14 days?
3. 近14日內同居者是否由國外入境或仍在14天隔離期滿後之7天自主健康管理期內且同居者尚未取得PCR核酸檢測陰性報告者?。
Have you ever close contacted cohabitants people who returned from aboard in the last 14 days or close contacted people who is under 7-day self-health management after 14-day quarantine and do not get the negative PCR test report?
4. 近14日內曾密切接觸被匡列之居家隔離者或14天隔離期滿後之7天自主健康管理者且被接觸者尚未取得PCR核酸檢測陰性報告者(密切接觸泛指距離小於兩公尺且時間大於15分鐘)?
Have you ever close contacted (distance less than 2 meters and duration more than 15 minutes) people who received a quarantine notification from CECC in the last 14 days or close contacted people who is under 7-day self-health management and isn't get the negative test report after 14-day quarantine?
5. 本人,同居者或密切接觸曾是否曾參與"旅遊泡泡"且尚處於"加強自主健康管理"期間者?
Have you, cohabitants or close contact with someone who take part in the "travel bubble" and are still in the "enhanced self-health management" period?
